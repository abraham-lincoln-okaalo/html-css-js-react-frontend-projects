What I do

Audit Planning and Execution

I lead the planning and execution of financial statement audits for clients. This involves understanding the client's business, assessing risk areas, developing audit strategies, and coordinating with the audit team to perform audit procedures.

Risk Assessment and Internal Controls Evaluation

As a Senior Audit Manager, I assess the effectiveness of the client's internal controls and evaluate their impact on the financial statements. This includes identifying control weaknesses, recommending improvements, and providing guidance on risk management.

Financial Reporting and Compliance

I ensure that the client's financial statements comply with relevant accounting standards and regulatory requirements. This involves reviewing financial statements, footnotes, and disclosures and providing guidance on complex accounting issues.
